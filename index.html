Our Features

Check out our list of awesome features below.

Master Calendaring

Schedule your rooms and resources with ease. We support multiple buildings and rooms, and even have an approval system in place!

Notifications

Get notified of event cancellations, upcoming events, and more!



Baptism Manager

Manage your baptisms easily with our custom workflow. Accept registrations, manage classes and attendance, and print out the paperwork for your records.

Bereavement Ministry

Allow bereavement ministers to fill out forms for the office and the sacristan, track all funerals, and notify anyone about the date/time of the funeral services. 

Event Registration

Allow events scheduled on the calendar to be open for registration, think of this as your RSVP system. We'll even help you process ticket sales!

Priest Appointments

Allow reception to book clergy for one-on-one appointments, cancel said appointments, or even reschedule them.
